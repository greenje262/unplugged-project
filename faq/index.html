Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

Registrants will be picked up by retreat faciltators and brought to the complex at an undisclosed time. It is recommended to have a bag packed and carried with you at all times.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

We are committed to providing a unifying and memorable experience for all involved, but we find that our retreats are most suited for teams with a diverse pool of blood types.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Our secure complex currently has beds for fifty individuals, but according to the fire marshals, we are allowed to house seven hundred twenty individuals before it constitutes a fire hazard. Please let us know the size of your team and we will be happy to work with you.

Is there really no wifi available?

There is wifi available, but only those with gold stars may access it. Gold stars are awarded to those who display remarkable resilience in our regularly-scheduled stress tests, and may also be distributed by facilitators as they see fit. Of course, the wifi is also available in the event of emergencies or unfortunate accidents.
